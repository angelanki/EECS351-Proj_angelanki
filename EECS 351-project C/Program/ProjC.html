User Guide

(Mac: F1 + fn; Windows: F1)

Button Controller

  • Press the button to run/stop the moving on screen.

Keyboard Controller

  • ←/ →/ ↑/ ↓ -> move eyeposition to left/ right and zoom in/ out.
  • A/ D/ W/ S -> look left/ right/ up/ down.
  • a/ d/ w/ s/ q/ e -> move light's position.
  • Z -> switch on/off the "headlight".
  • X/ x/ z/ c -> switch on/off cubeLight/ torusLight/ sphereLight/ cylinderLight.
  • 'esc' -> reset the screen.
  • 'space' -> run/ stop the moving.

Interactive switching between two lighting/ shading methods.

  • Press button to change Phong lighting/ Blinn phong lighting light

Mouse Controller

  • Drag your mouse to move texture balls.
